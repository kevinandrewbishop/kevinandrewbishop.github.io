Moon Lander with Bombs

Last year I was on a pygame kick and decided to create a little Moon Lander game. The moon has a randomly generated jagged surface and you can only land properly in areas that are flat enough. You've also got to land pretty softly or you'll crash.

Honestly it was pretty boring. So I decided to add moon bases with missile silos that fire at the lander. I also equipped the lander with bombs. The task now is to destroy as many silos as possible and land safely before running out of fuel. Much more fun!

This is written in python, not javascript, so you can't play in your browser. But if you've got python and pygame, just fork the repo and have fun!

Once you're finished with that, if you're really depserate try this Hangman game that I made to help me learn javascript/jquery.
