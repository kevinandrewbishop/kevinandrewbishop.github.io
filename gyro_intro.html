Gyro Game

The object of the gyro game is simple:

Move your player (the tiny black dot) to his home (the yellow block) without falling in the lava.


Play on mobile rather than desktop. Why?

The player moves as you tilt your phone. It moves sort of like a marble balancing on a piece of wood.

Each time you beat a level, the game gets harder!

Be sure to:
  • Hold your phone flat on its back
  • Turn off screen rotation
